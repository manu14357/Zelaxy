# Sponsors

Zelaxy is **free and open source** under the MIT license. Sponsorships help sustain active development, cover infrastructure costs, and keep the project growing.

## Why Sponsor?

- **Sustain open source** — Your support keeps Zelaxy maintained, updated, and free for everyone
- **Faster development** — More funding = more time dedicated to new features, blocks, and integrations
- **Community features** — Sponsors help fund community-requested features and improvements
- **Recognition** — Your name/logo appears across the platform, README, docs, and arena
